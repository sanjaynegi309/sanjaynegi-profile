Advanced & Material Focus Styles (Prompts 6-15)

Transform the subject into a chibi-style claymation figurine. The surface should have a slightly textured, matte finish with visible 'fingerprint' imperfections. Place it in a stop-motion-inspired miniature garden diorama. Use soft, diffused lighting.
Convert the image into a 1/10 scale collectible figurine carved from a single piece of translucent amethyst. The figurine should have sharp facets and internal light refractions. It's displayed on a minimalist, illuminated black light box, making the gem glow.
Turn the subject into an intricate 1/7 scale steampunk automaton figurine, constructed from polished brass and aged copper, with visible gears. It stands on a heavy, riveted iron base. Illuminated by the warm, flickering glow of a gas lamp.
