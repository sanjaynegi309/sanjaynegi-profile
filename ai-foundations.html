Not just Vibe Coding. This is Vibe Engineering.

Vibe coding is asking AI to generate code. Vibe engineering is using AI with clarity, structure, debugging discipline, and engineering judgment.

Vibe Coder

❌ Asks AI to generate code randomly

❌ Copies output without understanding

❌ Gets stuck when errors appear

❌ Builds messy prototypes

❌ Depends completely on the tool

❌ Says: “AI made it”

Vibe Engineer

✅ Gives AI clear specs and constraints

✅ Reviews and understands the output

✅ Debugs errors systematically

✅ Builds maintainable small projects

✅ Uses judgment, structure, and iteration

✅ Says: “I engineered this with AI”
